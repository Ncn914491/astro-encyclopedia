Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 25, 2025

Introduction

Astro Encyclopedia ("we," "our," or "the App") is an offline-first educational mobile application that allows users to explore space and astronomy content. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data.

Data We Collect

We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for advertising
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Payment information
  • Contact lists or personal files

Local Data Storage

The App uses local device storage (Hive database) to:

  • Cache astronomical data for offline access
  • Store user preferences (e.g., dark mode settings)
  • Save browsing history for improved user experience
Important: This data remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Cloudflare Workers

We use Cloudflare Workers as a proxy to deliver content from NASA's public image library. Cloudflare may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps) as part of their service. Please refer to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

NASA Image and Video Library

The App displays content from NASA's publicly available Image and Video Library. This content is in the public domain. Please refer to NASA's Media Usage Guidelines for more information.

GitHub Pages

Static data files are hosted on GitHub Pages. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for details on their data practices.

Offline-First Architecture

Astro Encyclopedia is designed as an offline-first application:

  • Core content is bundled with the App for immediate access without internet
  • Additional content is cached locally when downloaded
  • The App functions fully without an active internet connection
  • No user data is uploaded to any server

Data Security

  • All data remains on your device
  • No authentication or accounts are required
  • No sensitive permissions are requested (no camera, microphone, location, or contacts access)
  • The only permission required is INTERNET for downloading optional content updates

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The App is designed for educational purposes and is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You may clear the App's local cache at any time through the Settings screen.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:

  • GitHub Issues: github.com/ncn914491/astro-encyclopedia

Data Deletion

To delete all locally stored data:

  1. Open the App
  2. Navigate to Settings
  3. Tap "Clear All Caches"

Alternatively, uninstalling the App will remove all associated data from your device.

Summary: Astro Encyclopedia is a privacy-respecting educational app. We don't collect your personal data. Everything stays on your device.
